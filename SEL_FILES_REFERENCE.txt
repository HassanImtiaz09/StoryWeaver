DATABASE SCHEMA
===============

Table: sel_templates
- Stores SEL story templates
- 25 built-in templates (5 per competency)
- Fields: title, description, competency, age range, difficulty
- prompt_template for Claude, emotionalGoals (JSON)

Table: sel_progress
- Tracks story engagement per child
- Competency breakdown
- Records completion date

Table: sel_responses
- Emotional check-in responses
- emotion_felt, emotionIntensity (1-5), reflection
- Links to template and story arc
