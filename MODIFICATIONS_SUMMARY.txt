IMPROVEMENTS BREAKDOWN:
======================

IMPROVEMENT 1: Optimal Bedtime Story Length
Status: IMPLEMENTED
Files: server/_core/promptTemplates.ts
Impact: Age-appropriate story lengths that fit bedtime routines
Details:
  - Ages 2-4: 6 pages × 100 words = 5-8 min narration
  - Ages 5-7: 8 pages × 125 words = 8-12 min narration
  - Ages 8-10: 10 pages × 150 words = 12-18 min narration
  - Ages 11-13: 12 pages × 170 words = 15-20 min narration

IMPROVEMENT 2: Multiple Themes/Morals (up to 3)
Status: IMPLEMENTED
Files: app/new-story.tsx, server/_core/promptTemplates.ts, server/routers.ts
Impact: Rich educational content with multiple complementary lessons
Details:
  - UI supports selecting 1-3 morals from 12 options
  - Counter badge shows "X/3" selected
  - Backend accepts array, stores as comma-separated string
  - Morals injected into story context for Claude
  - New stories.generateStory endpoint created

IMPROVEMENT 3: Episode Consistency (Strengthened)
Status: IMPLEMENTED
Files: server/_core/promptTemplates.ts
Impact: Multi-episode stories maintain coherent narrative
Details:
  - Dynamic narrative phase guidance (Introduction → Resolution)
  - Series continuity requirements for character/setting consistency
  - Episode-aware story structure
  - Cumulative character growth across series

IMPROVEMENT 4: Narration Pacing & SSML Markup
Status: IMPLEMENTED
Files: server/_core/promptTemplates.ts, server/_core/elevenlabs.ts
Impact: Natural-sounding, paced narration optimized for sleep
Details:
  - Claude generates SSML break tags (<break time="Xs"/>)
  - Tone modulation markers: (softly), (excitedly), etc.
  - processNarrationMarkup() adjusts voice settings per tone
  - Validates break times don't exceed ElevenLabs limits
  - Applies to both per-page and episode-level audio

DATA FLOW DIAGRAM:
=================

USER SELECTION (new-story.tsx)
    ↓
    ├→ storyLength
    ├→ storyTone
    └→ storyMorals (1-3 items)
        ↓
        stories.generateStory()
        ↓
        educationalValue: "Courage, Kindness" (joined string)
        ↓
        storyArcs table (stored with educationalValue)
        ↓
        episodes.generate()
        ↓
        Parse: educationalValue → moralLessons array
        ↓
        customElements: { morals: [...] }
        ↓
        EPISODE_GENERATION_TEMPLATE
        ↓
        MORAL LESSONS section in prompt
        ↓
        Claude generates story with:
        - Age-appropriate length
        - Multiple morals woven naturally
        - Narrative phase guidance
        - SSML breaks & tone markers
        ↓
        generatePageAudio/generateEpisodeAudio
        ↓
        processNarrationMarkup()
        ↓
        Extract tone, adjust voiceConfig, keep SSML
        ↓
        ElevenLabs API (processes breaks natively)
        ↓
        Natural-sounding bedtime narration
